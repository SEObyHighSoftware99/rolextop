Akkad Consultants: local SEO work that improved visibility, keyword positions, and lead quality

Published: April 7, 2026 Updated: April 7, 2026 7 min read By Algorthmoptix
Local SEO Lead generation Consulting visibility
Business typeProfessional consulting firm with regional service demand.
Main goalImprove qualified visibility and create more useful inbound opportunities.
OutcomeTraffic improved, key terms reached page 1, and the site began producing valuable monthly calls.

This case study focused on a consulting website that was not getting enough relevant traffic and was struggling to convert visitors into inbound leads. The SEO work combined technical cleanup, local search optimization, keyword targeting, and conversion-aware page improvements.

In service businesses, raw traffic alone is not the full story. Relevant local traffic and valuable calls can matter far more than vanity metrics.

Starting challenge

The source document highlights three main issues at the start of the engagement: low organic traffic, limited inbound leads, and a website that was not sufficiently optimized for conversions.

Low trafficThe site was not ranking well for important industry terms.
Few leadsInbound opportunities were limited despite valuable services.
Low conversion efficiencyThe site experience needed stronger conversion support.
  • The campaign needed both visibility improvements and better conversion support, not just more impressions.
  • Regional relevance mattered, so local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization were included.
  • Mobile responsiveness and performance were treated as part of the user experience layer.

Strategy implemented

The work described in the case study took a broad but practical approach to service-business SEO.

Audit + on-page workWebsite audit, competitor research, keyword research, title and header optimization, and stronger internal linking.
Technical + local SEOMobile responsiveness, structured data, local targeting, and Google Business Profile improvement.
  • Service pages and content were updated to align better with the needs of the target audience.
  • Local keywords were targeted to help the firm appear for searches such as architecture-related terms in Abu Dhabi.
  • Off-page and link-building work supported stronger trust signals and wider visibility.

Results achieved

After the work was implemented, the case study reports that visibility improved in ways that mattered for the business.

+80%organic traffic growth reported in the source file
3–4 per monthquality leads and calls from the website
Page 1 termsfor key industry keywords
  • Organic growth: the case study reports an 80% increase in organic traffic.
  • Lead quality: the website began generating 3–4 quality leads and calls per month.
  • Ranking gains: key industry terms moved onto the first page, improving discovery and trust.

Why lead quality mattered

The source PDF makes an important point: even a small number of qualified calls can be valuable when project values are high. That is why this case study is useful for service businesses that care about deal value, not just raw visit counts.

Local intentThe campaign improved visibility for location-based searches and Google Business Profile results.
Conversion relevanceMore useful traffic matters more than broad, low-intent traffic for consulting offers.
Business impactThe case study frames calls and leads as meaningful opportunities, not just surface-level numbers.
Client satisfactionThe source file describes a clear positive return from the SEO work.

Takeaway

This is a strong example of why local SEO for service businesses should connect ranking work, user experience, and conversion intent. Better visibility matters, but better-fit leads matter even more.
